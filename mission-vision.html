Mission

“To spread the Good News of Salvation and the truths of the Bible, and bring lost souls to the Lord Jesus Christ — the Giver of Hope.”

Our church was established to give honor and glory to God; to make disciples of the Lord Jesus Christ; to build up men and women for the ministry and the proclamation of the full gospel message in the power of the Holy Spirit.

Our goal: To make disciples of the Lord Jesus Christ. A disciple is one who lives as His master lives. We aim not only to reach people but to make disciples who will disciple others. We cannot stop at our city — we participate in the universal effort to expand His kingdom to every people group.

One message: The full gospel of His Kingdom — not only deliverance from sin, but the abundant life God desires for His people today. Our method: Declaration and demonstration in the power of the Holy Spirit.

Source: Word of Hope – Mission Page

Vision

“To be a Christ-centered, Spirit-empowered, and Bible-based church that ministers to the whole family and reaches the world with the Gospel of Jesus Christ.”

We envision building a church with a strong foundation in God’s Word, a passion for winning souls, and a commitment to disciple believers who will impact society with Kingdom values.

Our vision is to see transformed lives, strong families, vibrant communities, and nations touched by the power of God through faithful discipleship and mission.

2025 Goals

  • Win 175,000 souls for Christ through MV Good News Island Evangelism, Gospel Buses Crusades, Kid’s Mobile Outreaches, and Church Services. Believing the Lord to supply & abundantly bless the church supernaturally.
  • Plant & establish 40 new satellite churches (with 5 in urban centers & 5 abroad). Train & equip satellite and marketplace pastors.
  • Reach 10,000 prayer partners – a church earnestly praying for revival, believers being used in the Gifts of the Holy Spirit. Establish prayer groups for all department ministries.
  • Intentionally build 1,000 new Life Groups. All ministry heads to lead Life Groups. Develop & encourage discipleship multiplication. Empower mature leaders to disciple and train new disciple leaders.
  • Develop a spiritually vibrant and growing Youth Ministry. Encourage young people to win other youth for Christ & provide relevant youth programs & activities.
  • Promote mission participation and exposure for all interested & able WOH members through short-term trips via MV Good News, Gospel Buses, trips at home or abroad for missions.
  • Establish an effective Life-Coaching & Counseling Ministry to help the hurting, those struggling with addictions or spiritual oppression to find God’s love, healing, forgiveness, & acceptance in the Body of Christ.
  • Provide leadership training to all present ministry leaders and future leaders. Promote & encourage enrollment at CGS, HLC & Hibs.
  • A vibrant church where all faithful members and families have strong relationships with the Lord Jesus Christ, experiencing His promise of “Abundant Life.” Encourage entrepreneurship for success and prosperity.
  • Improvement Projects: WOH Central’s Gymnasium, Auditorium, Chapel, and Paradise Prayer Garden, plus updating of equipment to improve facilities.
